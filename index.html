Biography

Tom Marvolo Riddle was a highly talented and ambitious student at Hogwarts School, known for his intelligence, charm, and extraordinary magical abilities. He was also a natural leader, often drawing his peers to him with his charisma and persuasive skills. Riddle demonstrated a deep curiosity about the magical world, eagerly seeking knowledge beyond the standard curriculum and often engaging in discussions about ancient spells and potion, making him a respected figure in the school.

Info

  • Birth: December 31, 1926
  • Hogwarts House: Slytherin
  • Known for: Exceptional magical abilities
  • Achievements: Head Boy, Award for Special Services to the School

History

Tom Marvolo Riddle uncovered the identity of a fellow student who had brought an Acromantula onto school grounds, which resulted in the creature attacking and killing a student. As a consequence, the responsible student was expelled from Hogwarts.
